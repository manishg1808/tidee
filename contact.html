Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get my clothes back?

Standard service takes 24-48 hours. We also offer express service (12 hours) and same-day service for urgent needs.

Do you provide doorstep pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer free doorstep pickup and delivery within Mumbai city limits. Additional charges apply for suburbs.

What if there's damage to my clothes?

We take full responsibility for any damage. Our quality control ensures this rarely happens, but we offer complete compensation if it does.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer bulk discounts starting from 20 pieces (10% off) up to 20% off for orders over 100 pieces. Contact us for custom quotes.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Mumbai and surrounding areas including Andheri, Bandra, Juhu, Powai, Goregaon, and Malad.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive tracking updates via SMS and WhatsApp at every stage of the process.

About Tidee

Your trusted partner for premium laundry and dry cleaning services, committed to quality, hygiene and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Tidee began with a simple yet strong belief — that everyone deserves fresh, clean, and perfectly laundered clothes without any hassle. Founded in 2020, Tidee started its journey in Noida as a small laundry service startup. At that time, we had limited resources, but a big vision to make the laundry industry better and more convenient.

What started as a local service has now grown into a trusted brand with thousands of satisfied customers. Throughout this journey, we have consistently prioritized high quality, strict hygiene standards, and customer satisfaction. By embracing modern technology, we have made laundry and dry-cleaning services faster, safer, and more convenient than ever before.

Today, Tidee is known for its premium laundry and dry-cleaning services. From doorstep pickup to fresh and timely delivery, we handle every process with complete responsibility, care, and precision — so you can stay worry-free and save valuable time.
